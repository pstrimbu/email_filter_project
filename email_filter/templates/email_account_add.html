It is an "app-specific password" and needs to be generated. For GMail accounts go to https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords, login with your gmail account, then 'create' and enter an "App name" of EmailMatchbox. Copy the resulting "app password" that will look like "hcsn obaf kznh gqfw". This is what you need to enter for your App Password. For Apple (me.com) accounts, go to https://account.apple.com/, login with your iCloud account (may need to confirm from another device) and go to "App-Specific Passwords". Click the plus icon, enter EmailMatchbox as the app name (may need to confirm your password), then copy the generated password (looks like "bkde-fxcx-pozc-zbmj"). This is what you need to enter for your App Password.
